Lambda Alpha Nu

About Us

We are Lambda Alpha Nu, the co-ed social fraternity for Computer Science majors at the University of Texas at Austin.

ΛΑΝ cultivates future startup founders, engineers at top tech companies, graduate students, teaching assistants, chefs, and most importantly, good people who are passionate about computer science. We are focused on social activities and building community in a laid back, enjoyable environment.

We will be accepting applications from all students who are interested in tech.
