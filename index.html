Lambda Alpha Nu

About Us

We are a co-ed social fraternity for Computer Science majors at the University of Texas at Austin. We will be accepting applications from all students currently pursuing a degree in CS. We are focused on social activities and building community in a laid back, enjoyable environment.

Contact Us

For any questions, feel free to contact the officers by emailing general@texaslan.org or reaching out to one of us directly. Rush related questions should be directed to rush@texaslan.org. For specific questions best suited for contact with our president, contact root@texaslan.org.

If you are from another university and are interested in opening your own chapter of Lambda Alpha Nu, please contact us at expansion@lambdaalphanu.org.

Be sure to follow us on Twitter and like us on Facebook to stay up to date.
