Lambda Alpha Nu

About Us

We are Lambda Alpha Nu, the co-ed social fraternity for Computer Science majors at the University of Texas at Austin.

ΛΑΝ cultivates future startup founders, engineers at top tech companies, graduate students, teaching assistants, chefs, and most importantly, good people who are passionate about computer science. We are focused on social activities and building community in a laid back, enjoyable environment.

We will be accepting applications from all students who are interested in tech.

Contact Us

For any questions, feel free to contact the officers by emailing general@texaslan.org or reaching out to one of us directly. Rush related questions should be directed to rush@texaslan.org. For specific questions best suited for contact with our president, contact root@texaslan.org.

If you are from another university and are interested in opening your own chapter of Lambda Alpha Nu, please contact us at expansion@lambdaalphanu.org.

Be sure to follow us on Twitter and like us on Facebook to stay up to date.
