Lambda Alpha Nu

About Us

We are a co-ed social fraternity for Computer Science majors at the University of Texas at Austin. We will be accepting applications from all students currently pursuing a degree in CS. We are focused on social activities and building community in a laid back, enjoyable environment.

Rush

We have several events planned over the next few weeks for the prospective members to get to know the founders and vice versa. We will be having two info sessions, whose time and location can be found on the rush calendar. We will be accepting applications starting on Monday 9/8, and a review and interview process will follow.
