About Us

What are we?

We are a co-ed social fraternity for Computer Science majors at the University of Texas at Austin. We will be accepting applications from all students currently pursuing a degree in CS. We are focused on social activities and building community in a laid back, enjoyable environment. We will have a simple rush and interview process where we will get to know you and determine if we think you will be a good fit.
