What are we

We are a social fraternity for Computer Science majors at the University of Texas. We are co-ed and accept applications from all students currently pursuing a degree in CS. We're focused on social activitys and building community in a laid back, enjoyable environment. We will have a simple rush and interview process where we get to know you and determine if we think you'll be a good fit (so basically don't be a total asshole).
